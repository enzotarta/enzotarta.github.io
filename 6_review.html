How to be a good reviewer

There are many resources you can look at in case you are reviewing a paper for a journal or a top conference.

Here below some useful resources for the ICCV 2023 conference.

  • Reviewer Guidelines, Ethics, and Tips
  • Set of slides "How to be a good reviewer"
  • For ICCV 2023, it is categorically forbidden to use of Large Language Models or online chatbots, such as ChatGPT, in any part of the reviewing process, for two major reasons. First, reviewers must provide comments that faithfully represent their original opinions. It is unethical to resort to Large Language Models to automatically generate reviewing comments that do not originate from the reviewer's own opinions. Second, online chatbots, such as ChatGPT, collect conversation history to improve their models. Therefore, their use in any part of the reviewing process violates the ICCV confidentiality policy.
  • A reviewer should be aware of what the author is expected to do: Author Guidelines and FAQ
